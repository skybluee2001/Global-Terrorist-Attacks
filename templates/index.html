GLOBAL TERRORIST ATTACK

My analysis is based on the Global Terrorist Attacks between 1970 and 2017. I have made statistical analyses and visualized data to draw valuable insights. I have analyzed the world data, followed by narrowing my focus on India. I have analyzed data according to the year, country, city, attack type, target type, attack group, etc., to explore the data's specifications and make necessary inferences. I have used Visualisation tools like Seaborn, Matplotlib, Folium. I have used the Flask web framework for the web frontend. Below are my statistical plots with the analysis!

Here is the distribution of the total number of attacks by the country. Iraq witnessed the maximum number of attacks closely followed by India.

Here is the plot of the total number of casualties in each country. Iraq was again witnessing the highest number of casualties, and India following it shortly.

Here is the distribution of the number of terrorist activities every year. There is a significant increase in terrorist activities in recent years, with the peak observed in 2014.

The distribution of the total number of casualties over the year. The maximum number of attacks is evident in 2016. The number of casualties has increased significantly in recent years.

Circular Statistical plot of the percentage of the Attack Type which was responsible for the casualties. Bombing/Explosion and Armed Assault are the cause of 73% of these attacks' casualties.

Here is the plot of the total number of attacks and total number of casualties in India's Cities. Srinagar witnessed maximum number pf attacks resulting in huge number of deaths.

Here is a HeatMap showing the magnitude of casualties of a particular year and city in India. The maximum casualties was observed in 1991 in India.

Here is the plot of the number of casualties in India every year. There were maximum number of casualties in 1991 in India.

Here is the Statistical plot showing the Target Type of the attack in India. Trains have been attacked the most in India, followed by Reserve Police Force Personals

Map showing the intensity of the terrorist attack at different locations across the world in 1991. The highest number of attacks were observed in the south-east part of North America.

Terrorist Attacks have witnessed a sharp increase in the recent year 2009-2017. The maximum number of attacks is observed in Iraq, shortly followed by India. In 1991, India had the maximum number of deaths across all the cities. In India, most of the attacks have been targeted on trains. With this, I would like to conclude my analysis!
